About Nolan

Christopher Nolan is a British-American filmmaker born on July 30, 1970, known for complex narratives, stunning visuals, and practical effects. He rose to fame with Memento and redefined superhero films with The Dark Knight Trilogy. His hits include Inception, Interstellar, Dunkirk, Tenet, and Oppenheimer, exploring themes of time, memory, and reality while blending intelligence with blockbuster appeal.
